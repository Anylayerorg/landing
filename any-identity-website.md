## 2. What Is `.any`

`.any` is the universal identity namespace of Anylayer.

It replaces fragmented wallet addresses, usernames, and credentials with a single human-readable identity that works across apps, chains, and platforms.

A `.any` name is not just a name — it is an identity anchor that carries reputation, verification, and trust.
